Four Main Goals of a Risk Assessment

  • Assets: identify, validate and classify
  • Risks: identify
  • Quantify: the impact of potential threats
  • Economic Balance: between the impact of the risk and the cost of the control.

  • Critical systems first, direct support second, indirect support last
  • Prefer production over non-production
  • Inconsistencies between production and simulation
  • Maintain acceptable level of risk throughout assessment
  • Testing can and does break things

The Exploitation Process

  • Someone identifies a vulnerability in an OS
  • Someone writes and exploit to compromise the system based on the vulnerability
  • The OS vendor releases a patch to fix the vulnerability
  • Customers apply the patch and mitigate, or expunge the vulnerability
  • Those who donâ€™t patch are exposed to the vulnerability and resulting exploit.

Exploitation Process Steps

  • Step 1: Identify the OS and Applications
  • Step 2: Find Vulnerabilities for OS and apps
  • Step 3: Find exploits associated with discovered vulnerabilities
  • Step 4: Exploit and plant back doors
